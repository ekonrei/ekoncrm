FAQ

Common
Questions.

Do I need my own GoHighLevel account? +
No. We set up and manage your sub-account inside our agency GHL. You get your own login, your own CRM, your own phone numbers — without needing to pay for GHL separately. Everything is included in your $119/mo.
Is A2P registration hard to set up? +
Not for you — we handle it entirely. A2P 10DLC registration is what allows your texts to actually reach sellers without getting blocked by carriers. Most people skip it and wonder why their campaigns stop working. We make sure you're compliant from day one.
What happens to my data if I cancel? +
Your contacts, pipeline, and data stay in the system and are exportable. We recommend exporting your contacts before cancelling. There are no cancellation fees and no lock-in — if you leave, you leave clean.
How does the usage billing work? +
Your $119/mo covers platform access. Outreach usage — SMS, calls, email — is billed separately at $0.01 per action from a wallet you fund. You only pay for what you send. There's no minimum usage and no surprise overage bills. You control your own spend.
Do I need to be tech-savvy? +
No. That's the whole point. We build everything for you — pipelines, automations, phone numbers, A2P, your seller website. You get on a 1-on-1 call with us, tell us your market and goals, and we set it up. Your job is to work leads and close deals, not configure software.
What's included in the cash buyer database? +
A pre-loaded database of verified cash buyers and real estate investors, filterable by market, buy box, and property type. You can also add your own buyers. The Buyer Match AI then cross-references your deal details against the database and surfaces the best fits automatically — no manual sorting required.
