Questions

Affiliate FAQ.

How much do I earn per referral?
30% of the plan price every month: $29.10/mo for an Executive referral ($97 plan) and $36.00/mo for a Hosted referral ($120 plan). You keep earning for as long as they stay subscribed.
Is the commission really recurring?
Yes. It's not a one-time payout — you're paid every month the referral remains active. Five active Hosted referrals is $180/mo, every month.
Is there a cap on referrals?
No cap and no expiration. Refer as many people as you want — the more active subscribers under you, the more you earn each month.
How and when do I get paid?
Commissions are paid out monthly. We'll cover exact payout details and your referral link when you're approved into the program.
How do I join?
Hit Apply now, fill out the quick application, and join our Discord. We review every application and typically respond within 48 hours with your link and next steps.
