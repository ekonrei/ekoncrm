The short version: EKON CRM collects the information you give us to operate the platform. We don't sell your data — ever. Questions? support@ekoncrm.com
01
Information We Collect
  • Name, email address, and phone number
  • Billing and payment information (processed via Stripe)
  • Business name and real estate operation details
  • Content you upload or create inside the platform
  • Communications with our support team

We also collect technical information including IP address, browser type, device identifiers, and feature usage.

02
How We Use Your Information
  • Provide, operate, and maintain the EKON CRM platform
  • Process payments and send billing communications
  • Send onboarding information, updates, and support messages
  • Monitor platform performance and fix technical issues
  • Improve and develop new features
  • Comply with legal obligations

We have no advertising program. Your data doesn't leave our ecosystem for targeting purposes.

03
Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information. We may share it only with:

  • Service providers: Stripe (payments), GoHighLevel (CRM), Twilio (SMS/calling) — all contractually required to protect your data
  • Legal requirements: If required by law or court order
  • Business transfers: In connection with a merger or acquisition
  • With your consent: In any other case you've authorized
04
SMS & Communication Consent

By providing your phone number, you consent to receiving SMS messages related to your account and service updates. If you use EKON CRM to send SMS to your own contacts, you are solely responsible for obtaining proper TCPA-compliant consent from those recipients.

05
Cookies & Tracking

We use cookies to keep you logged in, remember preferences, and understand platform usage. You can control cookies through your browser settings, though this may affect platform functionality.

06
Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including encryption in transit and at rest, access controls, and regular security reviews. No system is 100% secure — we will notify you of any breach affecting your data as required by law.

07
Your Rights
  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your personal information
  • Opt out of marketing communications at any time
  • Request a portable copy of your data

Contact support@ekoncrm.com to exercise any of these rights.

08
Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Continued use of the platform after changes constitutes acceptance.
