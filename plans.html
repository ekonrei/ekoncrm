Questions

Good to know.

Are there any setup fees?
No. Both plans are $0 setup. You pay your monthly price and that's it — the full build, onboarding, and A2P registration are all included.
Do I need an LLC and EIN?
Executive ($97/mo) is for people who already have an LLC and EIN — your A2P registers under your own entity. Hosted ($120/mo) requires neither — you operate under our registered business, so you can start without forming a company.
How long does A2P registration take?
We handle your A2P 10DLC registration and you're typically live in about 48 hours. Carrier review timelines can vary and aren't guaranteed, but most accounts are delivering quickly.
What is the power dialer?
A built-in dialer that lets you burn through your call list fast, with automated call dispositions — when a call ends, the lead is tagged and the right follow-up fires automatically. No manual logging, no leads slipping through.
How is usage billed?
Your monthly plan covers platform access. Outreach is billed at cost: roughly $0.01 per SMS segment, $0.0166 per calling minute, and email is included free. No markup.
Is there a contract? Can I cancel?
No contracts. Both plans are month to month and you can cancel anytime.
Can I switch plans later?
Yes. Start on Hosted with no LLC, then move to Executive once you've formed your entity — no rebuild and no disruption to your data or campaigns.
